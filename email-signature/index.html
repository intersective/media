Practera Email Signature Tool

Dear Practera User,

Follow these instructions to generate your own Practera email signature.

  1. Click on each signature detail to edit
  2. Replace each detail with your own information
  3. Press the "Copy to Clipboard" button
  4. Paste into the signature field of your email client

You may find instructions for inserting signatures for your email client in these links: Gmail/Google Mail, Apple Mail (macOS), Outlook, iCloud.com Mail. For other clients, it should be similar – paste it into the signature field.
